About Bunker

What is it?

Bunker is a contact storage application. It is built to hold things like addresses, birthdays, phone numbers, and emails.

How much does it cost?

Bunker is currently completely free. It is also open-source! You can find the entire source code for this project on gitHub. ;)

Why was Bunker created?

Bunker was made for a personal project at Union College during a web technologies course. Its main purpose was to teach the concepts and applications of node.js along with many of its accompanying plugins.

Who made Bunker?

Bunker was written nearly completely by Nathan Bland, a senior computer science major at Union College. With some key assists from the instructor of the class - Ben Barber - and some aid from fellow classmates.
